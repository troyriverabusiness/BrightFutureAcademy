Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions here

  • How big are the class sizes?

    Classes contain no more than 6 students so that the tutor can attain to everyone’s needs and doubts.

  • Does same tutor work with a student throughout the entire school year?

    Yes, the tutor groups remain the same throughout the school year.

  • How do you help students prepare for important exams?

    There are intensive exam preparation weeks arranged before important examinations. These are free-of-charge to help students tackle any potential last-minute doubts and problems.

  • What happens if a student needs to miss or reschedule a session?

    Students can catch-up to a maximum of 3 missed lessons in a school year free-of-charge.

  • Are there additional fees for materials, assessments, or special programs?

    A one-time registration fee of 100€ is charged at the start of every school year for registering with the academy. Additionally, depending on the subject, workbooks may be required for an additional cost (approx. 25€).

  • Can your tutors assist with college applications, including essay writing and interview prep?

    Bright Future Academy specializes only within the IB Curriculum. It does not offer any services in regards to university applications or interview prep.

  • Do your tutors have experience with the IB grading criteria and rubrics?

    Yes, our tutors are thoroughly familiar with the IB grading criteria and assessment rubrics. Many of our tutors have experience as IB examiners or teachers, giving them deep insight into how to maximize students' scores based on official standards.

  • Can you help with predicted grades and improving performance in mock exams?

    Yes, our tutors help students prepare for school mock exams, which often form the basis for predicted grades. We focus on addressing weak areas and provide strategies to improve performance, helping students achieve the highest possible predicted grades.

  • How do you prepare students for IB exams?

    We provide extensive exam preparation through past papers, mock exams, and timed practice tests. Our tutors also focus on exam techniques, question analysis, and time management to ensure students are fully prepared for the rigors of IB assessments.

  • Can your tutors help with Extended Essay (EE) and Internal Assessments (IA)?

    Yes, we provide guidance on choosing topics, conducting research, and structuring both the Extended Essay (EE) and Internal Assessments (IA). Tutors offer feedback throughout the writing process to ensure that students meet IB standards and criteria.

  • Can students book extra sessions during exam season or when they need more help?

    Yes. In case of extra sessions a student can notify their tutor, who will plan the extra sessions accordingly.
