Business Management HL

Business management examines business decision‑making processes across a range of organisational types and the impact of internal and external factors. Students learn to analyse, discuss and evaluate business activities at local, national and international levels.

Learning Objectives

Students will develop comprehensive understanding and skills in this subject area.

Understand how businesses are organised and influenced by their environment
Apply business tools and techniques to real and simulated problems
Analyse human resource strategies and leadership styles
Evaluate marketing, finance and operations decisions
